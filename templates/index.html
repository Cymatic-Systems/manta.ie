All-in-One Asset Management

Safe in the Cloud

Your creative assets are securely encrypted at rest, ensuring that your work is protected in the cloud. From concept art to final animations, every file is shielded, allowing you to concentrate on what matters most - creating. With MANTA Lab, your assets are safeguarded from unauthorized access, facilitating secure and seamless collaboration.

Update Assets with Ease

With MANTA Lab, updating assets is simple. Whether adjusting a model or perfecting an animation, you can upload new versions quickly and easily. A simple click shares the latest updates, keeping your team aligned and informed. Changes can sync seamlessly across devices, allowing everyone to access the most recent versions when needed. You have control over which files get updated, minimizing confusion and ensuring a smooth creative workflow.

Built Around Your Process

Manage all your creative files in one intuitive platform. MANTA Lab's organized asset browser lets you quickly find, sort, and categorize everything from 3D models to sound effects. Maintain clarity and efficiency in your projects with advanced filtering options, making asset management as smooth as your creative process.

Track Changes and Roll Back

Maintain a complete history of your asset modifications with MANTA Lab. Easily review past versions and comments to understand the evolution of your work. If needed, roll back to a previous version with a single click, giving you the flexibility and confidence to experiment while preserving your creative integrity.

Unified View, Compare, and Review

3D Asset Viewer

Streamline collaboration with our advanced 3D viewer. Comment on mesh topology, texture quality, and UV layouts while rotating and zooming in on assets. Provide precise feedback to ensure every detail meets your creative standards, enhancing the quality of your 3D models.

2D Asset Viewer

Simplify your 2D asset reviews with our intuitive viewer. Navigate through images and animations, leaving comments on color choices, composition, and effects. This tool fosters clear communication, making it easy to refine assets before final implementation.

Audio Asset Viewer

Review audio assets efficiently with our dedicated audio viewer. Comment on clips, dialogue, and sound effects to provide targeted feedback on volume, timing, and spatial effects. Enhance collaboration between sound designers and developers to ensure a cohesive auditory experience.

Effortlessly Visualize Changes

3D Comparison Tools

Easily compare versions of your 3D assets with our intuitive side-by-side viewer. Focus on specific annotated areas to highlight changes and improvements. Utilize animation playback features to slow down and loop sections, making it simple to spot differences and refine your designs effectively.

2D Comparison Tools

Compare your 2D assets effortlessly with layered side-by-side comparisons. Focus on annotated areas to see specific adjustments, and analyze frame-by-frame differences in animations to ensure every detail aligns with your vision. This tool simplifies the review process, helping you make informed decisions for your artwork.

Audio Comparison Tools

Quickly evaluate your audio assets with our streamlined comparison tool. Easily compare versions to assess changes in loudness, dynamic range, and peak levels. Select from various visualizers and loop small sections to pinpoint differences, ensuring your audio design meets the project’s quality standards.
